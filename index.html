About Me

I'm a high school student at the Theoretical Lyceum "Orizont" Durlești. Since 2024, I have been working as a volunteer in the Institute of Applied Physics in the Republic of Moldova, researching Mechanical Properties of Materials in the "Iulia Boiarskaia" Laboratory.

Fractal

In November 2024, Marius Cerlat and I co-founded the National Mathematics Contest "Fractal", organized monthly for students in grades V–XII. The contest features 3 categories: Seniors, Juniors, and Beginners. Learn more and find past problems and results at concursfractal.com. The English translations of the problems, for each category, can be found on the AoPS page of the contest: Fractal AoPS (Moldova Contests section).
